Passing

I am scared, but happy for new beginnings
I wonder about what will come next
I hear voices calling me
I see a light
I wish to go to the light
I am scared, but happy for new beginnings

I pretend to imagine running to this light
I feel the warmth of its yellow beams
I touch the light and feel at peace and in harmony
I worry that I will not see the light again
I cry thinking about leaving to follow the light
I am scared, but happy for new beginnings

I understand that this is a huge decision
I say to myself "everything will be ok"
I dream about the light for hours
I try to make an effort to go to the light
I hope for the best
I am scared, but happy for new beginnings
